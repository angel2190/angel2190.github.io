Privacy Policy

Last Updated: [16/08/2023]

This Privacy Policy describes how information is handled and used in our mobile application [SpaceJumper] in relation to Google Play Games leaderboards.

Collected Information

Our app uses Google Play Games leaderboards to allow players to compete in achievements and scores. By using this feature, scores and achievements are collected and displayed on a global leaderboard. We do not collect or store any additional personal information in our app.

Use of Information

The information collected by Google Play Games leaderboards is solely used to display scores and achievements on the global leaderboard and to enable social interaction among players.

Google Play Games Services

By using the leaderboards, you agree to the terms and policies of Google Play Games Services. We recommend reviewing the Privacy Policy and Terms of Service of Google Play Games Services for more information on how Google collects, uses, and safeguards information.

Security

We take reasonable measures to ensure the security of players' scores and achievements on the leaderboards.

Changes to this Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy occasionally to reflect changes in our practices related to leaderboards. We will notify you of significant changes to this policy by sending a notification through the app.

Contact

If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy in relation to leaderboards, please contact us at [spacejumperhelp@gmail.com].
